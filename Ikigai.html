Ikigai: The Japanese Secret to a Long and Happy Life

by Héctor García and Francesc Miralles

★★★★☆ (4.7/5)

Discover the Japanese concept of "ikigai" - a term that translates to "reason for being" - and learn how to find a purpose that brings joy and fulfillment. Through conversations with the residents of Okinawa, authors García and Miralles capture the lifestyle practices that contribute to longevity and happiness. These insights include staying active, connecting with nature, and fostering community, alongside the powerful practice of mindfulness.

With exercises to help you find your own ikigai, this book offers a practical guide to cultivating a life of purpose, health, and happiness.
